ABOUT THE WORKSHOP

Want to get a head-start on forming a team? Maybe you've never hacked before and you're a little nervous about where to start? Or maybe you need a little help coming up with an idea for the Hackathon? Well you've come to the right place. We put together a few Pre-Hackathon workshops to get you started. You can go to one workshop or hit them all. Your choice. Space is limited to 75 people so get your ticket early and arrive to the workshop early to secure your seat.

Hosted by Co-Founder of Startup League, Annee Ngo | Don’t have an idea or a team to work on just yet? Or are you looking to find the right team members to bring your idea to life? Join us for this facilitated networking workshop and find the right fit. Connect with likeminded people and begin brainstorming ideas.
